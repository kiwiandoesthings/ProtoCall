ProtoCall Chatting

By KiwianDoesThings

ProtoCall chatting is a small for-fun chatting app inspired by Discord and old IRC chat.
Currently it supports these features:

  • Accounts
  • Different message boards (rooms)
  • Persistent messages
  • Server-side authentication and security
  • Keeping track of rooms you've used

It is also still in development, and currently these things are being worked on

  • Keeping track of where you are (so you don't have to reconnect to a room every time you reload the page)
  • Private message boards between accounts
